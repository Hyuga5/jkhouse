FAQs
Common Questions for Nepal Buyers

Which pipe is best for drinking water in Nepal?

For drinking water in Nepal, food grade PPR pipe is the best option. It is NSF/ISO compliant, corrosion-free, and safe for hot and cold potable supply lines. Guess what, ITPF has it all.

What is the price of PPR pipe in Nepal?

PPR pipe price Nepal depends on brand, size and PN rating. Standard 20mm to 63mm PPR pipe from ITPF or Nepatop is priced competitively.

Which water tank brand is best in Nepal?

Hilltake water tank is widely trusted in Nepal for UV stabilized, food grade storage with strong layered walls.

What is the difference between PPR and CPVC pipe?

PPR pipe is primarily a green polypropylene pipe for hot and cold water, while CPVC is rigid, heat resistant and better suited for hot water bathroom use.

Do you supply pipes and fittings to contractors?

Do you deliver outside Kathmandu?
